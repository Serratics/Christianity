Beginner's Guide to Christianity

Basic Tenets

  • Belief in Jesus Christ
  • Saved by faith and works
  • Avoidance of sin as defined by the Bible
  • Relationship with God
  • Belief in the doctrine of the Trinity
  • Return of Jesus Christ
